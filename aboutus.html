About our Restaurant:

Tikka N' Talk is an Indian restaurant based in Forest Hills, Queens, NY, specializing in authentic Indian cuisines. We pride ourselves on serving traditional Indian dishes prepared with a mixture of exotic Indian spices to deliver an authentic taste of unique Indian flavors. All our dishes, such as Chilli Fish, Shrimp Tikka Masala, Shrimp Tikka, Kadai Paneer, Chana Masala, Kadai Murgh, are made with the freshest and local ingredients. Our Basmati rice is slow-cooked and served with the entrées of your choice. Our menu consists of extensive vegetarian and non-vegetarian choices. All our meat choices are made with 100% Halal meat. We also serve many vegan food items as per customer’s preferences. Our Garlic Naan, Chicken Tikka Masala, Chicken Tikka, Soya Chops, Lasooni Gobhi, Salmon Tikka, Shrimp Tikka Masala, Murgh Biryani, Seekh Kabab, and Vegetable Samosa are some of our most popular dishes in Queens, NY. We offer both dine-in and home delivery options for our customers so they can enjoy our authentic mouthwatering Indian cuisines whenever and wherever they want. You can place your order online through our website.
